Price Guide

The price of each cake is determined by two pieces of information: the size and the toppings.

Cake Sizes

Cakes are available for order in four different sizes. The size of the cake determines the base price of the cake. Only once size can be chosen per cake.

Diameter Base Price
6" $15.00
8" $20.00
10" $25.00
12" $30.00

Toppings

The toppings consist of any additional edible design elements added to the top of a cake. Multiple toppings can be chosen for each cake and the price for each topping will be added onto the base price of the cake.
